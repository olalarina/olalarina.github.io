Welcome!

This page is everything but perfect… Or let’s say, it looks like drunken butterflies flew over...Yet!

These are my first steps, more like baby steps in creating a personal html page without using any professionally built templates… I will get there… There will be thrilling and witty stories, next-to-professional pictures, and useful advices.

Personal pages are mirrors of life. They are more than CVs, they are constantly changing, dynamic summaries of achievements, falls, epiphanies and adventures.

It is also a chance for me to reflect on my life – is it a blank page or a novel, is it a page with vivid pictures and colorful blocks or more a long one-pager written in Times New Roman size 11?

My only regret is that I haven’t started the personal page earlier.

I am passionate about live... Love everything classical, but also enjoy experimenting...

You will find me running along the river after work or reading a book in a park, running (again) to the gate in the airport or enjoying a glass of champagne in a bar.
